# Ekota Football Club Management App

The live site is hosted at: **[armaank7.pythonanywhere.com](https://armaank7.pythonanywhere.com)**

## How to Access

Just head to the link above and you will be taken to the landing page. This link is valid for any web-based browsers and devices. From there you can either log in as a staff member or as a player.

Staff accounts can be registered through the registration page, but you will need the access code to create one. If you are part of Ekota's staff, you will have been given this code. For supervisors and markers testing the app, the access code is EKOTA2026

Player accounts are created by staff through the players hub, so players do not register themselves. Once a staff member has created your account, you can log in with the username and password they gave you. You will be asked to change your password on first login.

## What You Can Do

**As Staff:** Create, view, update and delete players, matches and training sessions. Record match appearances and training attendance. View the analytics dashboard with charts and stats for the whole club or individual players.

**As a Player:** View your own performance stats, match fixtures and training schedules. Update your email through your profile page.

## Tech Stack

Built with Django, Python, SQLite, HTML/CSS and Chart.js. Hosted on PythonAnywhere.
