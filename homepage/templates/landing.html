What the app covers

Player Management

Add and update squad members, positions, and personal details. Staff can create player accounts and manage the full squad.

Match Tracking

Log fixtures, results, and individual player appearances. Track goals, assists, and minutes played across all competitions.

Training Sessions

Schedule sessions, record attendance, and add coaching notes. Players can view their own training session history.

Performance Analytics

View goals, assists, appearances, and training attendance. Tables and Charts give a clear picture of player and club development over time.
